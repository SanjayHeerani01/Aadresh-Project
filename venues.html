Grand Thar Hall

Capacity: 300-500 guests Size: 10,000 sq ft Type: Indoor

Our largest and most luxurious venue, the Grand Thar Hall is inspired by the majestic Thar Desert of Sindh. This opulent space features high ceilings adorned with intricate chandeliers, traditional Sindhi motifs, and state-of-the-art amenities.

The hall can accommodate up to 500 guests in comfort, making it perfect for grand wedding celebrations, valimas, and large receptions. The spacious dance floor, elevated stage, and premium sound system create the perfect environment for memorable celebrations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common queries about our venues

How far in advance should I book my venue?

We recommend booking at least 6-8 months in advance for peak wedding season (October-March). For off-season dates, 3-4 months advance booking is typically sufficient, though popular dates can book up quickly year-round.

Are outside vendors allowed?

Yes, we allow outside vendors for specific services. However, we do have preferred vendor lists and some venue packages include certain services. Additional fees may apply for outside catering. Please discuss your needs with our event coordinator.

What happens in case of bad weather for outdoor venues?

For all our outdoor venues, we offer backup indoor options in case of inclement weather. This contingency plan is discussed during the booking process, and we monitor weather forecasts closely as your event approaches to make timely decisions.

Can we customize the venue decorations?

Absolutely! While our venues showcase traditional Sindhi décor elements, we welcome customizations to match your specific theme or preferences. Our event designers can work with you to create a personalized ambiance that reflects your vision while honoring cultural elements.

What is the payment structure for booking a venue?

Our standard payment structure requires a 30% deposit to secure your date, with an additional 40% due three months before the event and the remaining balance due one month prior to your event. We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and cash payments.
